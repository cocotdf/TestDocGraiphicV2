<h1>Licensing SOTA</h1>

<p>Our licensing system provides access to the entire toolkit suite included in SOTA. <strong>As part of this suite, by activating your license, you will be able to enjoy all the functionalities offered by LabVIEW</strong> <strong>Deep Learning toolkit.</strong></p>

<h2>License types</h2>

<p>We offer several types of licenses depending on your needs and usage.</p>

<h3><strong>Trial License (by default)</strong></h3>

<p>When you install <strong>SOTA</strong> for the first time, a <strong>30-day trial license</strong> is automatically activated on your machine.<br/>Provides access to all features before purchase.<br/>Ideal for discovering the toolkits and testing hardware compatibility.</p>

<p>
<h3><strong>Deployment License</strong></h3>
<p>Allows an end user to run an executable that contains a SOTA toolkit.<br/>Required to redistribute an application built with a Perpetual or Professional license.<br/>Does not include development features or access to LabVIEW palettes.</p>
<p>
<h3><strong>Professional License</strong></h3>
<p>Full license for industrial or commercial use.<br/>Includes all features, access to advanced toolkits, and priority support.<br/>Recommended for companies and integrators using SOTA in production.</p>
<p>
<h3><strong>Perpetual License</strong></h3>
<p>Lifetime license linked to a specific LabVIEW version.<br/>Grants full access to the toolkits.<br/>Cannot be used on a different LabVIEW version.</p>
<p>
<h3><strong>Developer (Dev) License</strong></h3>
<p>License reserved for official Graiphic partners.<br/>Contact Graiphic for more information about access conditions.</p>
<p>
<h3><strong>Academic License</strong></h3>
<p>Completely free license for students.<br/>Provides access to SOTA toolkits for educational and non-commercial purposes.<br/>To request one, please contact us at <a href="mailto:licence@graiphic.io">licence@graiphic.io</a> and include a copy of your student ID.</p>
</p></p></p></p></p>

<h2>License installation method</h2>

<p>We offer two methods to activate our SOTA license :</p>

<ul>
<li>The <strong>first method</strong> is the certificate generation, which involves an email-based file exchange procedure.</li>
<li>The<strong> second method</strong> involves entering a token that we will provide you with.</li>
</ul>

<p>In this section, we provide guidance on how to ask, install and activate your licenses. Please choose the appropriate method based on your requirements and follow the instructions provided.</p>

<h2>How to install and activate a licence ?</h2>

<p>rThis section provides step-by-step instructions on how to activate your SOTA suite with a certificate<strong>.</strong></p>

<h2></h2>

<h2>To begin, please follow these steps (Certificate)</h2>

<ol>
<li>Launch SOTA and log in (You must be logged in to install a license).</li>
<li>In the SOTA interface, locate and click status page in the Licence section.</li>
<li>In status, you can see if a license is active (By default, you have a 30-day license)</li>
<li>To create a license file, you need to provide the unique certificate specific to each computer.</li>
<li>To do this, you need to locate and click on Certificate in the License section.</li>
<li>Send us the generated file by email to <a href="mailto:contact@graiphic.io">contact@graiphic.io</a> (We will send you the license as soon as possible)</li>
<li>Once you have received the license by email, return to SOTA, locate and click on install in the license section.</li>
<li>Locate and click on Manage, in the License section.</li>
<li>Here you can manage your licenses and activate or deactivate them.</li>
</ol>

<p><strong>Congratulations and welcome inboard, your license is now activated.</strong></p>

<p><a href="https://www.youtube.com/embed/oxA_phq3mwA?feature=oembed">Get Started - Licence Certificate Installation</a></p>

<p>This section provides step-by-step instructions on how to activate you SOTA suite with a token<strong>.</strong></p>

<h2>To begin, please follow these steps (Token)</h2>

<ol>
<li>Launch SOTA and log in (You must be logged in to install a license).</li>
<li>In the SOTA interface, locate and click Redeem token page in the Licence section.</li>
<li>Take and enter your token and click on add license</li>
<li>Here, in Manage page, you can manage your licenses and activate or deactivate them.</li>
</ol>

<p><strong>Congratulations and welcome inboard, your license is now activated.</strong></p>
